India is predominantly an agriculture based country where 70% of the land is used for cultivation. Information Technology can be used gainfully to improve the agricultural sector. The aim of the project is to develop an Application that suggest remedies to the infected fruit(diseased), using an expert system approach.

In the present day, information technology is mainly providing solution at the corporate level. But through history, India is known as an agriculture country. So information technology should also consider the agriculture sector of the country. In the past days farmer faces difficulties in diagnosing the diseases during the cultivation process and lacks the ability in finding solution in a scientific manner. So he tries consulting the nearest horticulture office for solutions. This process of finding solution is time consuming and the farmer has to travel all the way from his place to the horticulture office which is usually located in urban areas. In recent years internet plays a very important role in area of network. Internet has the greater advantage of providing services at any geographical area in a short span of time. Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) are being widely used in the Agriculture Sector enabling access to information, resources, and market for improved agricultural productivity. Farmers can make use of the service by sending the image (ex: infected fruit, leaf etc) to the plant clinic. The image is compared in the server through expert system, the remedies are sent back efficiently.
